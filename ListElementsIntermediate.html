Connor's Recipe for Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 3 pounds of Chicken Wings
  • 3 tablespoons of Corn Starch
  • 1 tablespoon of Kosher Salt
  • Wing Sauce of choice

Instructions:

  1. Pat chicken wings dry
  2. Add corn starch and salt
  3. Bake at 450 for 1 hour
  4. Coat wings in sauce of choice
